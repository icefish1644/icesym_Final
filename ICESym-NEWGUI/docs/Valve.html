Valve

This object models a valve in the simulation.

List of Attributes

  • Label: Choose a label for this object to identifies it in object-tree and in Post Process.

  • Type of Valve: Type of valve: intake or exhaust.

  • Number of Valves: Number to divide the valve.

  • Type Data: Lift valve type: Exponential, Squared sine-wave or User-defined (it actives Lv Define).

  • Open Angle: Valve opening angle [deg].

  • Close Angle: Valve closing angle [deg].

  • Diameter Valve: Head valve diameter [m].

  • Lv Max: Maximum valve lift [m].

  • Valve Model: Toyota or Alessandri.

  • Lv Define: Two-column array with the valve lift data.

  • Discharge Coefficient: Discharge coefficient data.
